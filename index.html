A Breath of Fresh Air
in Mining
Mankind is dependent on mined minerals for a wide range
of uses integral to our subsistence. From houses to
hospitals, to automobiles to roads, to computers to
satellites many of man's critical daily needs are met by
mined resources.
However, with environmental concerns, compliance issues,
operational hazards, corruption, information asymmetries
and several other issues plaguing the industry, mining is
broken in many ways. With the Clean MIning Company, we
aim to fix it.
Our Core Values
Our children are our future, and our greatest assets. If there is one metric that can measure Clean Mining Company's performance against the goals we have set for ourselves, it is the extent to which our operations help leave a planet that is more sustainable for our children and the generations to come
All rights come with responsibility. When we are given permission to use minerals and natural resources that might have taken millions of years to be formed, we do not take lightly the responsibilities that come with it. We consider ourselves responsible to governments, local communities, and all stakeholders including our employees, at all our locations.
When we extract or use natural resources from a particular region, we believe it is most sustainable, fulfilling and fair when we share the returns from our operations there, with the local community. This philosophy has not only helped us build stronger communities in our areas of operation, but also allowed us to grow sustainably and steadily.
The impact of mining and the extent to which it is appropriate in each context is often a subjective opinion. This makes the regulatory environment exceptionally important in defining the boundaries within which we can operate in each location. Our commitment to the benchmarks we set for ourselves in terms of legitimacy and legal compliance, far exceeds industry standards.
The metals and mining industry worldwide is rather opaque. Data available is deficient. This not only leaves communities with a sense of distrust, but also limits the advances afforded by big data analysis and artificial intelligence. We aspire to bring transparency into the industry.
We are dreamers passionate about disruptive innovation, and at every step, we focus on identifying and using the latest technologies in transforming for the better, an industry that we believe has not seen enough technological transformation over the years. We believe we can be a catalyst in a digital revolution in the global mining industry.
